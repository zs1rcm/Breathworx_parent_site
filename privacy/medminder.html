Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

In short: MedMinder is a free medication reminder app. Your medication data is private and stays on your device. We don't sell your data, we don't run ads, and we don't share your information with anyone.

1. Who we are

MedMinder is operated by Breathworx ("we", "us", "our"). This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use MedMinder on iOS, Apple Watch, or the web.

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at privacy@breathworx.org.

2. What data we collect

Medication data you enter

  • Medication names, dosages, and forms
  • Scheduling information (times, frequency, duration)
  • Notes you choose to add
  • Adherence history (whether you marked doses as taken, missed, or skipped)

Family sharing data (optional)

If you choose to share medication reminders with a family member or caregiver, the information you share will be visible to them. You control what is shared and can revoke access at any time.

Device information

The app uses standard device capabilities (notifications, timers, Apple Watch complications). We do not collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, or location data.

3. How we use your data

We use your data only to:

  • Deliver medication reminders to you on time
  • Track your adherence history so you can review it
  • Sync your data across your devices if you enable iCloud sync
  • Enable family sharing if you choose to use it

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any form of behavioural tracking.

4. Where your data is stored

All medication data is stored locally on your device. If you have iCloud enabled and choose to sync, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account, which is managed by Apple under their own privacy practices.

We do not operate any servers that store your personal medication data.

5. Data sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent your data to anyone. MedMinder has no advertising partners, no data brokers, and no third-party analytics trackers. Your medication information is personal and stays that way.

6. Third-party services

MedMinder uses the following Apple-provided services:

  • iCloud — optional; used for sync and family sharing; subject to Apple's privacy policy
  • Apple Push Notifications — used for local reminders only
  • Apple Watch Complications — used to display reminders on your watch face

7. Your rights

Because your data stays on your own device, you are in full control. You can at any time:

  • Edit or delete any medication entry
  • Clear your adherence history
  • Revoke family sharing access
  • Delete the app to remove all associated local data
  • Request information about any data we hold by emailing us

8. Important disclaimer

MedMinder is a reminder tool, not a medical device. It does not replace professional medical advice, and its reminders should not be relied upon as the sole means of ensuring medication adherence. Always follow the instructions of your healthcare provider and the medication packaging.

9. Children's privacy

MedMinder is not directed at children under 13. If a parent or guardian uses MedMinder to manage a child's medications, the parent or guardian is responsible for the data entered into the app.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Significant changes will be communicated within the app.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact privacy@breathworx.org.
