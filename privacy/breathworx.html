Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

In short: Breathworx is a free app. We don't sell your data, we don't run ads, and we don't track you. Your breathing session data stays on your device (and in your iCloud if you enable sync).

1. Who we are

The Breathworx app is operated by Breathworx ("we", "us", "our"). This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use the Breathworx app on iOS, Apple Watch, or the web.

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at privacy@breathworx.org.

2. What data we collect

Breathworx is designed to collect as little data as possible. Here's what the app handles:

Data you enter

  • Breathing session history (patterns used, duration, completion)
  • Streak and progress data
  • Personal preferences (preferred patterns, session length, sound settings)

Health data (optional)

If you grant permission, Breathworx can read from and write to Apple HealthKit to log mindful minutes and breathing sessions. You can revoke this permission at any time in your device settings. Health data is stored by Apple and is never sent to our servers.

Device information

The app uses standard device capabilities (haptics, notifications, timers). We do not collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, or location data.

3. How we use your data

We use your data only to:

  • Provide the core functionality of the app (guided sessions, history, streaks)
  • Remember your preferences between sessions
  • Sync your data across your devices if you enable iCloud sync

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any form of behavioural tracking.

4. Where your data is stored

All session and preference data is stored locally on your device. If you have iCloud enabled and choose to sync, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account, which is managed by Apple under their own privacy practices.

We do not operate any servers that store your personal data.

5. Data sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent your data to anyone. Breathworx has no advertising partners, no data brokers, and no third-party analytics trackers.

6. Third-party services

Breathworx uses the following Apple-provided services:

  • Apple HealthKit — optional; subject to Apple's privacy policy
  • iCloud — optional; subject to Apple's privacy policy
  • Apple Push Notifications — used for local reminders only

7. Your rights

Because your data stays on your own device, you are in full control. You can at any time:

  • Delete individual sessions or your entire history from within the app
  • Revoke HealthKit permissions in your device settings
  • Delete the app to remove all associated local data
  • Request information about any data we hold by emailing us

8. Children's privacy

Breathworx is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Significant changes will be communicated within the app.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact privacy@breathworx.org.
