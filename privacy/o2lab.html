Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

In short: O2Lab handles health information for oxygen therapy training. We take data protection seriously, we don't sell data, and clinicians are responsible for obtaining appropriate patient consent before entering any personal or health data.

1. Who we are

O2Lab is a free web app operated by Breathworx ("we", "us", "our"). It is designed for clinicians and trainers to track treadmill-based oxygen therapy sessions for their patients.

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at privacy@breathworx.org.

2. Who can use O2Lab

O2Lab is intended for use by healthcare professionals, trainers, and individuals managing their own oxygen therapy training. It is not a medical device and is not a substitute for professional medical advice.

3. What data we collect

Account data

  • Email address and password (for user accounts)
  • Name and role (clinician, trainer, or individual user)

Patient and training data (entered by the user)

  • Patient identifiers (name or coded reference as chosen by the clinician)
  • Training programme details (target flow rates, duration, intensity, goals)
  • Session logs (date, time, O2 flow rate, duration, distance, user-reported response)

Technical data

  • Standard web server logs (IP address, browser type, access timestamps) for security and troubleshooting

We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking scripts.

4. Clinician responsibility

If you are a clinician or trainer using O2Lab to track patient data, you are responsible for:

  • Obtaining the appropriate consent from patients before entering their information
  • Complying with applicable data protection laws in your jurisdiction (such as GDPR, HIPAA, or local equivalents)
  • Using anonymised or coded patient references where possible
  • Safeguarding the credentials used to access the app

5. How we use your data

We use your data only to:

  • Provide the core functionality of the app (logging sessions, tracking programmes, generating trend analysis)
  • Authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • Maintain the stability and security of the service

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or behavioural tracking.

6. Where your data is stored

Data entered into O2Lab is stored on secure servers. Access is restricted to authorised personnel for maintenance purposes only. Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest.

7. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. We will only disclose data if required by law or to respond to a valid legal request.

8. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data associated with your account
  • Correct or update your data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data in a standard format
  • Object to any processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact privacy@breathworx.org.

9. Data retention

We retain your data only as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app settings or by contacting us.

10. Security

We use industry-standard security practices including TLS encryption, secure password hashing, and access controls. However, no method of transmission or storage over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Children's privacy

O2Lab is not intended for use by children. If a clinician uses O2Lab to track training for a minor, the clinician is responsible for obtaining consent from a parent or legal guardian.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.

13. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact privacy@breathworx.org.
