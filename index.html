Breathworx

iOS Apple Watch Web

Guided breathwork for calm, focus, and resilience

Science-backed breathing exercises delivered through guided sessions. Whether you need to calm anxiety, sharpen focus, or build a daily breathing practice — Breathworx adapts to your needs with real-time guidance on your wrist, phone, or browser.

  • Guided breathing patterns (Box, 4-7-8, Coherent, and more)
  • Real-time haptic guidance on Apple Watch
  • Session history and streak tracking
  • Calm ambient soundscapes
O2

O2Lab

iOS Apple Watch

Track your free-flow oxygen training on the treadmill

O2Lab helps you log and track your treadmill training sessions with free-flow oxygen. Manually record your session data — flow rates, duration, distance, and how you felt — and watch your progress over time.

  • Manual logging of O2 flow rate, duration, and intensity
  • Treadmill session tracking and history
  • Progress charts and training trends
  • Simple, distraction-free interface

MedMinder

iOS Apple Watch Web

Never miss a dose again

Simple, reliable medication reminders across all your devices. MedMinder helps you stay on track with prescriptions, supplements, and vitamins — with gentle notifications that actually work, and a clean interface that anyone can use.

  • Flexible scheduling (daily, weekly, as-needed)
  • Apple Watch complications and notifications
  • Medication history and adherence tracking
  • Family sharing for caregivers
